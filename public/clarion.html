About

Clarion off-dock Terminal is a bonded terminal which currently serves EUKOR Car Carriers, Five-Star Logistics and other shipping lines to reduce their Vessel waiting time, improve space management and ensure quick turn-around time. The terminal is fully operational with all facilities that make it a one-stop Customs off-dock Terminal in Nigeria. Apart from our well experienced staff with adequate shipping skills, we are strategically positioned for all port operations. With all the facilities in our shipping company and Terminal, it will be combined to serve our clients and partners well to ensure that vessels waiting time is drastically reduced even during port congestion period. Clarion Bonded Terminal is an international logistics firm, and a member of the Clarion group of companies, a dynamic and innovative company relying on people, processes and technology to provide superior services to our customers. As the name implies Clarion off-dock is a Custom Bonded Inland Cargo Terminal, located within the vicinity of Tincan Island Port. With its capacity of about 6000 tiues, the terminal is highly invaluable to the policy drive of the Federal Government of Nigeria of making the Lagos Ports the hub of shipping in West Africa through ensuring an efficient turn around time for vessels. Clarions expertise is laid on a world class efficient IT system, efficient use of vehicle carriers to ensure improved safety, a reliable and proactive international network and a close relationship with the major operators of the international transportation. We are a world class logistics provider. Clarion off-dock Terminal jointly with the Nigeria Customs Service and the Nigeria Port Authority and other stakeholders provide warehousing and logistics solutions to corporate and individual customers. As member of the Clarion Group, we also provide end - to - end logistics solutions and support. We have invested heavily in our people, processes and technology to ensure that we provide excellent and unparalleled service delivery to our customers. A member of the Clarion Group, we are in a position to make the most of our experience and our network to continue to deliver high-quality services and logistics solutions..
